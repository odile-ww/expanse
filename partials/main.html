The Expanse

Terrific space opera written by James S.A. Corey

The Expanse is a series of realistic sci fi novels and short-stories written by James S.A. Corey. Set in a distant future, when people have colonized most of our solar system & are setting out to go beyond its limits, the saga is a perfect mix of "food for thought" and thrilling adventure.

The series is being adapted for television by SyFy, with the first season having debuted at the end of 2015.

Main Characters

Each work in the series has a few POV characters, who are also main characters in the story. However, across all novels it is the crew of the independent frigate Rocinante (Roci) that find themselves affecting the course of.
